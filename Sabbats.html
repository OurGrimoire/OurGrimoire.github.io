Wiccan Sabbats and Calendars

Wiccans generally practice eight different holidays, or sabbats, every year. The Wiccan year begins on the 31st of October and continues until the 30th of October. Therefore the sabbats will be listed in this order.

Samhain

Yule

Imbolc

Ostara

Beltane

Litha
