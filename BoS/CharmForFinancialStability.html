Charm for Financial Stability

This spell should be cast on the day or night a new moon. The spell was originally intended to be cast during the month of January to gain the benefits of the astrological energy of the month, however the spell can be cast on any new moon if needed.

What You'll Need

A piece of dark blue silk or voile (or similar)
A painting or photo of moving water
Blue agate or lapiz lazuli, two pieces
A glass vessel
Three stems of clovers (the more leaves the better)
Amber, one piece

Instructions

Begin by placing the blue silk or voile on a small table. This calls to the element Water which symbolizes the West and prosperity. Hang the painting or photo of moving water. This represents the continual flow of money into your household.

Place the two pieces of blue agate or lapis in the glass vessel and cover them in water so they are half submerged.

Place the cloverstems in the western most location in your home for three days and three night to encourage financial stability.

Carry the piece of amber to promote your positive presence in any environment.
